PRIVACY NOTICE

This is the privacy notice for Guardian bot the discord application run by the Præpositi project, this describes how and why we collect the data used by Guardian in its day to day operations,  use of our product indicates that you were sent our privacy notice and that you understand & agree to it. 

What data do we collect?
We collect certain data from your discord account such as discord id, and from the discord id’s we collect other information based on refinement techniques within the application we provide, the following list has full information on what data we collect and for what purpose it's used:


Discord ID, which is used to gather the rest of the information [this information (your discord ID) is saved permanently, users can not opt out of providing this information]


Common servers, we use our bot to check for the same ID in all of the server its in and list them all once during the detection cycle [This info is not saved at all under any circumstance]


Number of statistically similar ID’s within common servers,we use this info to help detect burner accounts [This info is not saved at all under any circumstance]


Date of account creation, we use this data within our detection cycle to ensure your account is not a burner account  [This info is not saved at all under any circumstance]


 
Important notes:
We do not save most of the information we use within our detection cycle and only use it once to ensure your privacy and safety


We use account ID’s as the foundation for our detection method and ban system, this information is publicly available and can be obtained easily via developer mode within the discord app.


We do not and will not provide access to our database to anyone under any circumstance  [a single exception will occur if discord requests a check to ensure we save the data we claim to save]
